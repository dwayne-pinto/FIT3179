The Air We Breath

6.7 Million
deaths each year from exposure to ambient and household air pollution
2.4 Billion
people primarily rely on polluting fuels and technologies for cooking

From smog hanging over cities to smoke inside the home, air pollution poses a major threat to health across the globe. Almost all of the global population (99%) are exposed to air pollution levels that put them at increased risk for diseasesincluding heart disease, stroke, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, cancer and pneumonia.
